10. ROBREDO, LENI

Maria Leonor Gerona Robredo (born on April 23, 1965 in Naga City, Camarines Sur), popularly known as Leni, is an economist, lawyer, and politician serving as the 14th vice president of the Philippines until June 30, 2022. She is the wife of the late Interior Secretary Jesse Robredo.
