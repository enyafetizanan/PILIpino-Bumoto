01. DE GUZMAN, LEODY

Leodegario de Guzman (born on July 25, 1959 in Mabini, Naujan, Oriental Mindoro), popularly known as Ka Leody, is a labor organizer and politician. He is the chairman of Bukluran ng Manggagawang Pilipino and the party leader of Partido Lakas ng Masa.
